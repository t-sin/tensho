天書

What is this?

Ideographic space invader game.

The original concept, using kanji as sprites, is proposed by Toh Enjoe (円城塔) the author. The concept appears in the book "Mojika" (文字渦). One of its theme is characters, not letters. A lot of ideas about characters are illustrated in the book.

How to play

It's simple. Usage is like this:

  • 'A' key: move left
  • 'D' key: move right
  • 'J' key: shot, start game

Destroy them all!!!
